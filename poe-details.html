Path of Exile 2 retains the series' hallmark isometric perspective and dungeon-crawling mechanics, introducing a host of enhancements. The game boasts a new skill system featuring 240 active skill gems and 200 support gems, providing players with unparalleled customization options. With 12 character classes, each offering three unique ascendancy paths, the depth of character development is substantial. Combat is dynamic, incorporating elements like dodge rolls for improved mobility and a variety of weapons, including spears, crossbows, and flails. The endgame is robust, featuring an infinite atlas of monster-infested maps, challenging boss fights, and a plethora of loot, ensuring that players have ample content to explore and conquer.
