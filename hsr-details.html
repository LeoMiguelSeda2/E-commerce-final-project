Honkai: Star Rail is a free-to-play role-playing gacha video game developed and published by miHoYo. It is the fourth installment in the Honkai series, utilizing some characters from Honkai Impact 3rd and some gameplay elements from Genshin Impact.

ADD TO CART
  • Game ID: HSR

Setting & Story Set in the Honkai Universe, but it’s not a direct sequel to Honkai Impact 3rd. You play as the Trailblazer, a customizable protagonist who awakens aboard a space station. The player is connected to a mysterious force called the Stellaron. You join the crew of the Astral Express, a space train, to explore different planets, resolve crises, and uncover the secrets of the universe. Key themes: Space exploration, cosmic threats, ancient civilizations, and factions with opposing ideologies.


Gorgeous anime-style graphics. Deep lore & worldbuilding. Unique combat compared to other HoYo games (no action combat, just strategy). Regular updates with new characters, story chapters, and events.
