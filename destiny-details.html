MMORPG first-person shooter developed by Bungie. Set in a mythic science fiction universe, players take on the role of Guardians, protectors of humanity’s last city, who wield elemental powers to defend against hostile alien forces. The story follows Guardians as they battle the mysterious and dangerous alien threats like the Fallen, Hive, and Cabal. Players explore various planets, engage in both PvE (Player vs. Environment) and PvP (Player vs. Player) modes, and level up their characters. The game features missions, strikes, and raids that challenge teams of players to complete objectives and defeat powerful bosses. Guardians can customize their loadouts, choosing from a variety of weapons, armor, and abilities tied to one of three classes: Titan, Warlock, or Hunter. The game also includes seasonal events and expansions, which introduce new storylines, gear, and activities. Whether playing solo or in a group, Destiny 2 emphasizes teamwork, strategy, and continuous progression.
